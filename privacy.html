Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Sevans Services Ltd, trading as SEvans Advisory. We are registered in England and Wales under company number 16759055. Our registered address is 8 Kingston Close, Abingdon, Oxfordshire, OX14 1ES.

You can contact us at silvia@sevansadvisory.com with any questions about this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

Contact form submissions. When you complete the enquiry form on our website, we collect your first name, last name, email address, organisation name, area of interest, and any information you provide about your challenge.

Email correspondence. If you contact us directly by email, we will retain that correspondence.

Website analytics. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymised information about how visitors use our website, including pages visited and source of visits. This data does not identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and discuss potential engagements
  • Maintain records of client and prospective client communications
  • Improve our website and understand how it is used
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Legitimate interests. Processing enquiry and contact data to respond to potential clients and manage our business relationships.

Legal obligation. Where we are required to retain records to comply with applicable law.

5. Cookies

Our website uses Google Analytics which sets cookies to collect anonymised traffic data. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

Enquiry data is retained for up to 3 years. Where an engagement follows, data is retained for 7 years in line with standard business record-keeping requirements.

7. Third Parties

We use Google Analytics to analyse website traffic. For more information see Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

We use Formspree to process contact form submissions. Your data is transmitted securely and used only to deliver your message to us.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties without your explicit consent.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, erase, object to, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to request portability of your data.

To exercise any of these rights please contact us at silvia@sevansadvisory.com. We will respond within one calendar month.

9. Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.
